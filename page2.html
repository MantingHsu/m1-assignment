About Us

Welcome to DTLA Animal Adoption!We are dedicated to finding loving homes for animals in need. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that each animal is well-cared for until they find their forever home.

Our Mission

To rescue and rehabilitate animals, both big and small, and to improve animal welfare in our community. We strive to create lasting bonds between pets and their new families.
